p → %
e → em

You can use aliases instead of full units:

w100p → width: 100%
m10p30e5x → margin: 10% 30em 5ex


lh2 → line-height: 2;, fw400 → font-weight: 400;


m10 → margin: 10px;

m1.5 → margin: 1.5em;

m1.5ex → margin: 1.5ex;

m10ex20em → margin: 10ex 20em;, 
m10ex-5   → margin: 10ex -5px;.


bdrs → border-radius: ;
